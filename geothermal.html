Geothermal energy is heat within the earth. Geothermal energy is the slow decay of radioactive material within the Earth. This form of energy is renewable because heat is always being produced inside the Earth. Heat is also created from the friction between tectonic plates. The temperatures found here can range anywhere from 392 degrees fahrenheit at the Earths crust to 10,800 degrees fahrenheit at the Earth's core.
