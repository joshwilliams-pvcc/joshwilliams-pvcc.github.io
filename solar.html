Solar energy is any type of energy generated by the sun. It is becoming the fastest growing and most affordable source of new electricity. Solar energy is created by capturing light with photovoltaic panels that is absorbed from the sun. These panels use fusion to create energy. Fusion occurs when protons of hydrogen atoms violently collide in the sun's core and fuse to create a helium atom. Over time the cost of solar energy has become more afforable.

There are many benefits to solar energy:
  1. Solar energy is renewable and sustainable
  2. It is widely available
  3. Solar energy can be used for both electricity and heat
  4. It reduces fossil fuel conumption
  5. The Sun is free!
  6. Solar energy is efficient
  7. Solar panels can be installed most anywhere
